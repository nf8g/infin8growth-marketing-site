Why we started

Every business we worked with had the same problem underneath different problems. The knowledge that made things run was trapped in people's heads, in inboxes, in processes nobody had time to document.

AI changed what was possible. Not as a set of tools to bolt on, but as a new operating layer. One that could actually hold and surface the knowledge that makes a business run, and connect it to the work that needs doing every day.

That's the gap we exist to close.
