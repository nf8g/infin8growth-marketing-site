Why we started

Every business we worked with had the same problem underneath different problems. The knowledge that made things run was trapped in people's heads, in inboxes, in processes nobody had time to document.

AI changed what was possible. Not as a set of tools to bolt on — as a new operating layer. One that could actually hold and surface the knowledge that makes a business run, and connect it to the work that needs doing every day.

That's the gap we exist to close.

How we work

The operating system

Three layers. Each one makes the next one possible. This is the structure we bring to every engagement, regardless of industry.

01

Your knowledge

Your business knowledge captured and made searchable — the Company Brain. Processes, decisions, client histories, strategy. The foundation everything else is built on.

02

Your connections

One platform that pulls together everything your team already uses. Email, documents, CRM, calendars, finance tools. One place to work from.

03

Your workflows

The intelligent systems that save real time. Built on your context and your connections. That's what makes them work where generic tools don't.
