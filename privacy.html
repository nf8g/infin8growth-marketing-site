Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Infin8 Growth is an AI implementation consultancy operated by Marshall Tuten and Louisa St. Aubyn. Our website is https://infin8growth.ai. You can reach us at hello@infin8growth.ai.

We operate across the United States and United Kingdom. Where this policy references UK GDPR, it applies to personal data collected from individuals in the UK and European Economic Area.

What data we collect and why

Email address — Field Notes newsletter

When you submit your email address via the Field Notes subscribe form on our website, we collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending you our weekly newsletter, Field Notes. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for their marketing purposes.

Legal basis (UK/EU GDPR): Consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by emailing us directly.

Name — Foundation Assessment booking

Our booking page (hosted by Calendly) may collect your name and email address to schedule a Foundation Assessment call. This data is processed by Calendly under their own privacy policy. We use the information only to prepare for and follow up on your call.

Website analytics — Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. GA4 collects anonymised usage data including pages visited, time on site, and approximate location (city/country level). No personally identifiable information is collected via GA4 without your consent.

You can opt out of GA4 tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Email platform and data storage

Our newsletter is sent via a third-party email platform (currently being finalised — this notice will be updated once a platform is confirmed). Your email address is stored on that platform's servers. We use the platform only to send Field Notes. All platforms we use are required to comply with applicable data protection law.

We do not store your email address in any other system.

How long we keep your data

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to Field Notes. If you unsubscribe, your email address is deleted from our active mailing list within 30 days. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@infin8growth.ai.

Your rights

If you are in the UK or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Withdraw consent for processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@infin8growth.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics 4 to measure traffic and usage. These cookies do not identify you personally. By continuing to use this site, you consent to this use. You can disable cookies in your browser settings or opt out using the link above.

We do not use any advertising, tracking, or social media cookies.

Third-party links

Our website contains links to third-party services including Calendly, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting data to them.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our operations change. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We will notify Field Notes subscribers of any material changes via email.
